Malta special with Dimitar Berbatov

 podcast

Welcome to another episode of the UTD Podcast. This time we're in Malta with Dimitar Berbatov! In this episode, we learn about this football-loving country and why it has such strong links to English football and Manchester United. We visit the famous Manchester United Supporters' Club in Malta (the oldest in the world) to watch a match and meet the people that make it so special. We also learn about domestic football in Malta with Hibernians and Malta national team player Bjorn Kristensen. Thank you to Visit Malta for making this podcast possible.
